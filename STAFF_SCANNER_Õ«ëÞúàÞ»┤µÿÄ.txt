Staff address:

This opens member-admin.html.

IMPORTANT: Google Apps Script must also be updated:
1. Open the Apps Script project connected to the Club Google Sheet.
2. Replace its code with GOOGLE_APPS_SCRIPT_CLUB.gs from this ZIP.
3. Deploy > Manage deployments > Edit > New version > Deploy.
4. Do not create an unrelated new Sheet. Keep the existing Club Sheet.

Manager settings:
- Open club-manager.html
- Settings > STAFF PIN
- Save the PIN staff will use.

How staff uses it:
A. Open staff.html and enter Staff PIN, then tap QR-Code scannen.
B. On iPhone, the most reliable method is also: open the normal Camera app, scan the customer QR, tap the link, enter PIN, see green/red result, then confirm redemption.

Green = valid; tap Einlösung bestätigen.
Red = invalid/redeemed/expired; do not serve the reward.

The scanner supports:
- Member QR: shows member and lets staff add +1 paid drink.
- Offer QR: validates Welcome/Loyalty reward and confirms one-time redemption.
